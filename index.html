    Who we are

    Designed by scientists, for scientists

    We are formed of a committed group of biotechnology professionals with a combined 35 years experience in genomic healthcare. We understand the pressure on laboratories to deliver progress and our products are designed to get you there. Between us, we’ve got global experience of genomic healthcare from the NHS in the UK through to the Qatar genome project.

    What we stand for

    True innovation that benefits patients

    We translate research into routine diagnostic tools that directly benefit patients and scientists. Our non-invasive cell-free solutions improve diagnostic accuracy while saving time and improving the patient’s experience. Our research & development team are constantly validating and testing new cfDNA products.

    Cell3 Direct

    With no DNA extraction required, Cell3 Direct, reduces cost and saves time in Rhesus D diagnosis- under three hours from sample to result. Longer and wider ranging cell stability than other products on the market.

    Cell3 Direct

    For collection and stabilization of whole blood for circulating cell free DNA (ccfDNA) applications.

    Our Cell3TMPreserver is for the collection and stabilization of whole blood for circulating cell free DNA (ccfDNA) applications. It allows you to stabilize whole blood for longer and at a greater range of temperature than ever before. It’s easy to use with standard draw equipment and is made from plastic to avoid glass breakages.

    • Stabilizes whole blood for up to 21 days at room temperature.
    • Long and wide ranging cell stability (4°C - 37°C)
    • Plastic evacuated tube with standard tube format.
    • Easy to use with standard draw equipment and disposables.
    • Barcoded labels allow seamless workflow integration with automation
    • Validated for use in Non-invasive applications – NIPT / Liquid Biopsy
    • Validated for use in multiple downstream applications qPCR, NGS and DNA fragment analyser.
